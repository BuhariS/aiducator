How to read this page

How each metric is evaluated

Open this guide to see exactly what each number means.

View definitions

Completion rate

The percentage of enrollments with every lesson marked complete and a CourseCompletion record.

Assessment average

The average of confirmed teacher scores across all submitted assessment attempts.

Pass rate

The percentage of confirmed assessment scores at or above the course pass mark.

Need help

The number of distinct enrolled learners with needs-support progress or remediation/support attempts.

AI overrides

Confirmed grades where the teacher changed the AI recommendation.

AI-human gap

The average absolute point difference between AI suggestions and confirmed teacher scores.

Common mistakes

Repeated error themes extracted from AI grading feedback, ranked by frequency.

Drop-off rate

For each lesson, 100 minus completed learners divided by learners who started it.

Lesson time

The total minutes recorded while learners have that lesson open, limited to valid sessions.
